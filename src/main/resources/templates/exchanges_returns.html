Exchanges & Returns

Exchanging or returning an item couldn’t be easier. Fit your item in your home and let us know straightaway if you’d like a different size or a refund and we’ll collect from your home, work or an alternative address.

You have 28 days from receiving your order to:

  • Create your exchange or return in your CDF account
  • Book your complimentary collection
  • Send your purchase back to us

Items must be returned to us unused with all CDF tags and packaging still attached. Returns that do not meet our policy will be sent back to you.
